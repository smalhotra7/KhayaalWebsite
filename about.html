About The Festival

Promoting one of the richest and oldest musical forms in the world and giving a stage to those who perform it.

South Asian classical music is an art form that spans thousands of years and several countries. It is practiced and taught by the old and young alike, across the world even today. Yet, our larger community remains unaware of the depth and beauty South Asian classical music has to offer, and those who practice it find it difficult to establish themselves as performing musicians. It is in this vein that Khayaal was created at Georgia Tech - to promote awareness of South Asian classical music and to give a stage for young performing South Asian classical musicians. The festival debuted on November 16th, 2013, and now in its second year, it plans to increase its scope and continue to establish itself as one of the most unique musical experiences in the Atlanta area.

Forge a path for your music and perform alongside the youngest and most talented South Asian classical artists in the area.

Auditions for Khayaal 2015 are now closed. Check back soon for results!

Khayaal is designed to promote the youth of South Asian classical music. Our mission is to bring them a stage to showcase their talent, and to spread awareness of the art form to the community at large. If you are a musician wanting to perform at Khayaal, this is your time. Read the guidelines and click the button below to complete your audition application.

Who's Eligible

As you know, one of Khayaalâ€™s intentions is to promote and enhance the vast array of youth musical talent in the greater Atlanta area. For this reason, applicants must be under the age of 30 to audition. For any queries regarding this, please contact think@khayaalmusic.com.

Submission Deadline

All applications and video submissions must be received by the Khayaal board by 11:59 PM, December 12th, 2014. Auditions that are submitted on or before December 5th receive 2 free show tickets in addition to all performer benefits.

Submission Guidelines

1) Khayaal is an South Asian Classical music festival. We are looking for musical acts strongly rooted in a classical form (Hindustani, Carnatic, or a combination). Fusion acts are allowed, but we'd like for them to demonstrate a strong tie to South Asian Classical music in some way. For queries regarding this, please contact think@khayaalmusic.com.

2) Video audition submissions must be no more than 5 minutes long.

3) Video audition submissions can either be a link on YouTube, Vimeo, etc or in a video file (.mp4, .mov, .wmv, etc). A YouTube/Vimeo/etc link is highly preferred.

A Note on Accompaniment

If you have accompanists in mind for your audition, either percussive or melodic (ex. tabla and harmonium), please make every effort to submit your audition with them in the video. In the case that you don't have accompanists in mind, please do audition by yourself, and in the case you are accepted, we can figure out accompaniment for you.
